‚ú® Quick Panning
Hold Space and drag, or use your Middle Mouse Button to quickly pan around large canvases without switching tools.
üîç Precision Zoom
Use Ctrl/Cmd + Scroll Wheel for fine-grained zoom control (4% increments) when you need precise viewing levels.
üéØ Multi-Page Workflow
Use Ctrl + Arrow Left and Ctrl + Arrow Right to quickly navigate between pages. Organize your reference materials across multiple pages for better workflow management.
üñºÔ∏è Asset Viewer
Double-click any image on the canvas to open it in a separate Asset Viewer window where you can copy, save, convert formats, resize, or crop the image.
üöÄ Drag & Drop Everything
